Australia

Why Australia ?

Australia is a global education powerhouse with fantastic long-term opportunities. The infrastructure, facilities, teaching and networking experience in Australia is second to none. Moreover, the Australian government has been heavily involved in building a ‘truly internationalised’ education system that is driven by research to address the challenges of the future. Australian Universities has resulted in Australia being the 3rd most popular destination for international students.

Exams for Australia?

Students have to pass one or more additional tests before they can be considered by most universities for admission. These range from the standardised graduate and college admission tests such as IELTS – International English language Testing System, Pearson Test of English (PTE), Graduate Record Exam (GRE),Cambridge Advanced English (CAE) Certificate
